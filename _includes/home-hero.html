Buster Douglas

Category: Sports | Length: 1:26:43

James "Buster" Douglas is an American former professional boxer who competed from 1981 to 1990, and 1996 to 1999. He is best known for his stunning upset of Mike Tyson on February 11, 1990 in Tokyo to win the undisputed heavyweight title.
